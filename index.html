SAAHO

Saaho is a 2019 Indian action thriller directed by Sujeeth.It stars Prabhas as an undercover agent entangled in a high-stakes power struggle.The film features Shraddha Kapoor as a cop and love interest, adding both action and romance.Set across international locations, it boasts grand visuals and stylized action scenes.The story unfolds with twists, betrayals, and a hidden identity at its core.Saaho is known for its sleek presentation, heavy action, and mass entertainment appeal.

OG

They Call Him OG is a 2025 Indian Telugu-language action crime film, written and directed by Sujeeth, and produced by D. V. V. Danayya. The film stars Pawan Kalyan as the retired gangster OG, who returns to Bombay in 1993 after a ten year disappearance to confront the rival crime-lord Omi Bhau, played by Emraan Hashmi. The supporting cast includes Priyanka Mohan, Arjun Das, Sriya Reddy and Prakash Raj.

SALAAR

Salaar is a 2023 Indian action-thriller directed by Prashanth Neel.The film stars Prabhas as Deva, a powerful warrior with a mysterious past.Set in a violent, dystopian world ruled by rival factions, the story explores themes of loyalty and revenge.With gritty visuals and intense action sequences, it delivers a cinematic adrenaline rush.Shruti Haasan plays the female lead, adding emotional depth to the narrative.Salaar combines raw power, stylized action, and dramatic storytelling into an epic saga.

Mirai

Mirai is an Telugu superhero film starring Teja Sajja as Vedha Prajapati, a divine warrior.He plays a “Super Yodha” chosen to protect sacred scriptures using the powerful staff called Mirai.Trained in ancient combat like Karra Samu, he battles dark forces threatening the world.Manchu Manoj appears as the menacing villain Black Sword, creating a clash of epic scale.The film blends Indian mythology, fantasy, and futuristic elements with high-end visuals.Mirai positions Teja Sajja as a bold, mythic superhero in a first-of-its-kind Telugu cinematic universe.

Devara

Devara: Part 1 is a 2024 Telugu action film directed by Koratala Siva and stars Jr NTR in a dual role.Set in the coastal region of Erra Samudram, the story revolves around crime, smuggling, and revenge.Jr NTR plays both Devara and his son Vara, continuing a legacy of justice and rebellion.Saif Ali Khan plays the antagonist Bhaira, adding intensity to the narrative.The film features high-octane action, strong visuals, and a gripping score by Anirudh Ravichander.
